About Us

Training body responsible for Rail workforce training and development needs of Kelana Jaya Line, Ampang Line, Monorail Line and MRT SBK Line. 

Core Responsibility

To manage all systems and subsystems competency trainings including competency assessment, certification and licensing needs for staff required to operate and maintain the safety critical system and equipment. 

Facilities

4 Training Centres located at respective depots
